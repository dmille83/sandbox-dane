Biography:

Dane Miller serves as the Database Developer and an IT Application Support Specialist at Kansas State University's Global Campus. His primary responsibilities include developing and maintaining database applications, as well as documenting tasks routinely performed by Global Campus staff, and wherever feasible, automating them to boost efficiency and productivity.

Prior to working with Global Campus through OME, Dane worked at Kansas State University's IT Helpdesk for five years, providing him with a strong background in customer communication. His specialty lay in translating high-level technical details into metaphorical explanations that could be understood at a given customer's comfort level. 

In 2014, Dane caught the attention of Kansas State University's program developers in the Office of Mediated Education after volunteering to help develop a Scantron file-reader for the Kansas State University's newly-adopted Canvas learning management system, demonstrating proficiency in HTML, PHP and Javascript.

Shortly prior to graduation Dane began working with OME as a database developer, serving both K-State Global Campus and Kansas Child Care Training Opportunities, Inc.

Dane Miller has a bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems.
